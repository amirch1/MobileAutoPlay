Video autoplay (inline) for iOS mobile devices

The following demo shows automatic video playback on iPhone and iPAD.
Playback is muted and plays inline on iPhone as well.

Features:

  • Idle for online ads
  • Playback is muted
  • Clicking the video resume regular playback with audio from the current position

Limitations:

  • No audio
  • Doesn't work on Android devices
